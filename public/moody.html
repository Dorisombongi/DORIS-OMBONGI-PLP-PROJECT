How to Lift Your Mood

Exercises to Help You Feel Better

1. Dancing

Put on your favorite music and dance! It’s a fun way to release endorphins and improve your mood.

2. Deep Breathing

Practice deep breathing exercises to calm your mind and reduce stress. Inhale deeply through your nose, hold, and exhale slowly.

3. Gratitude Journaling

Writing down things you’re grateful for can shift your focus and improve your mood. Spend a few minutes each day reflecting on the positive.

4. Go for a Nature Walk

Spending time in nature can significantly boost your mood. Take a stroll in the park and enjoy the fresh air.
