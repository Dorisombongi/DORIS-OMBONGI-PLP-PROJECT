How to Relieve Cramps

Exercises to Help You Feel Better

1. Childâ€™s Pose

This gentle yoga pose helps relax the lower back and can relieve cramps. Kneel on the floor, sit back on your heels, and stretch your arms forward on the ground.

2. Supine Butterfly Stretch

This stretch opens the hips and relieves tension in the abdomen. Lie on your back with the soles of your feet together, letting your knees fall out to the sides.

3. Pelvic Tilts

This exercise helps strengthen the pelvic area and relieve cramps. Lie on your back with your knees bent, then gently tilt your pelvis upward.

4. Seated Forward Bend

This pose helps stretch the spine and abdomen. Sit with your legs extended, and gently reach for your toes, keeping your back straight.
