How to Boost Energy

Exercises to Help You Feel Energized

1. Light Cardio

Engaging in light cardio, like walking or cycling, increases blood flow and boosts energy levels. Just 10-15 minutes can make a difference!

2. Yoga Sun Salutations

This flowing sequence of poses can energize your body and mind. It helps improve circulation and flexibility, leaving you feeling refreshed.

3. Jumping Jacks

A great way to get your heart rate up quickly. Aim for a minute of jumping jacks to boost your mood and energy levels.

4. Stretching

Simple stretches can alleviate tension and invigorate your body. Focus on your neck, shoulders, and back to release stress.
