How to Relieve Bloating

Exercises to Help You Feel Better

1. Cat-Cow Stretch

This gentle stretch helps release tension in your abdomen. Start on your hands and knees, arch your back, then round it, alternating between the two positions.

2. Seated Forward Bend

This yoga pose can help ease bloating by stretching the spine and promoting digestion. Sit with your legs extended and reach for your toes.

3. Supine Twist

This twist helps massage your digestive organs. Lie on your back and bring one knee across your body to the opposite side.

4. Wind-Relieving Pose

A great way to relieve gas and bloating. Lie on your back and hug your knees to your chest, gently rocking side to side.
